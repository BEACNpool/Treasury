Why reserves trend down

On Cardano, reserves are designed to decrease over time: monetary expansion (new ADA used for rewards) ultimately comes out of reserves. That creates constant downward pressure even when other effects offset it.

What we publish here is receipts-first: observed reserves balance changes (from ada_pots) plus the explicit drivers we can attribute today (pot transfers via pot_transfer, and net deposits/refunds via tx.deposit). The largest missing driver is rewards-by-epoch (from the reward table), which we are materializing separately because it’s huge on mainnet.
